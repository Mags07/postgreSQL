About Us


Our head chef and the owner have worked together since 1998, and together they founded the Seafood Brasserie in 2009, in Crosshaven, Cork, Ireland. We have a passion for the local seasonal food and showing appreciation to our local fishermen. We are a small 25 seats restaurant, and welcome bookings with special occassions.
